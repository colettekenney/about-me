Where I'm From

I'm originally from a very small town in Manitoba called St. Adolphe. That's where I grew up. We moved to the city (Winnipeg) when I was 14 and that's where I went to university, to get my first degree in Mechanical Engineering

When I graduated, I moved right away to Calgary, Alberta. And for 15 years I have called it home. In the past three years we've been spending more and more time at our recreation property in Golden, BC. And this is where I love to be! Living full-time in Golden is WHY I am becoming a full-stack web developer. So I can work from here, and still make really good money, while being around all the things I love (see list above!)
